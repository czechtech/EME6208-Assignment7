I am a Computer Science and Technology Teacher at F. D. Roosevelt High School in Hyde Park, New York and a student in the University of Florida’s Computer Science Education program. I am a Chapter Leader for New York’s Mid-Hudson Valley Region, a member of CSforNY promoting K-12 CS Education across the state, and have served on multiple National CSTA Committees. In my community, I enjoy facilitating STEM activities for Girl Scout Troops and families to broaden participation in computing education.

This webpage is intended to showcase some of my digital skills.
